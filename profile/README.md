## 🖌 Overview  
Camstasia Advanced is an all-in-one screen recording and video production application built specifically for creating tutorials, product demonstrations, training content, and educational videos. It combines a capable recorder with a purpose-built video editor in a single, focused workflow.

The recording engine captures your screen at full resolution with simultaneous webcam and system audio input, giving you everything needed for a polished instructional video in a single recording session.

The timeline editor is designed around the needs of tutorial creators. Annotations, callouts, zoom-and-pan effects, lower thirds, and chapter markers all have dedicated tools that produce polished results without requiring video editing experience.

Quiz and interactivity features let you embed questions and surveys directly into your video content, making Camstasia Advanced a complete e-learning production tool rather than just a video editor.

For educators, trainers, product teams, and content creators who produce instructional video regularly, Camstasia Advanced is the production environment built around exactly that purpose.
